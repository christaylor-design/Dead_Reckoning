SURVIVAL GUIDE

The Objective

Clear the deck and survive. You win when the deck is empty and quotas are met.

Inventory

You must pick up items (Hand/Backpack) before using them. Drag Zombies directly to Action Zones.

The RUN Button

One-time use. Shuffles scavenge area back into the deck for fresh items.

Healing & Repair

Drag Hearts or Diamonds to the Scavenger card or health bars.
