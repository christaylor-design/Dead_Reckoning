SURVIVAL GUIDE

The Objective

Clear the deck and survive. You win when the deck is empty and the board is clear, provided you met your quotas.

Inventory Management

Items in the Scavenge Area must be moved to your Hand (Inventory) or Backpack (Storage) before they can be used. You cannot fight or heal directly from the floor!

Combat (Zombies)

  • Weapons: Drag a Weapon from your Hand onto a Zombie on the board.
  • Engagement: Drag Zombies directly to the Action Zone cards to fight or hide.

The RUN (1x) Button

Feeling overwhelmed? Click the orange **RUN** button in the top-right to shuffle current scavenge items back into the deck and draw 4 new ones. **One use only per game.**

Supplies & Health

  • Backpack: Store Food/Materials for quota. Items can be dragged back to your hand if you have space.
  • Healing: Drag Hearts from your Hand to the bars or Scavenger card.

Hunger Tax

Survivors must eat. -3 Player HP is applied at the end of every hand.
